## What This Is (and What It Isn't)

This is **not** a bank in the regulatory sense. It doesn't hold deposits, issue loans, or operate under a banking license.

This **is** a community financial infrastructure — a set of tools, protocols, and ledgers that let a community exchange value, pool resources, and support each other financially without going through institutions that profit from gatekeeping access to money.

---

## The Four Pillars

### 1. Mutual Credit Ledger
A mutual credit system creates money by mutual agreement. When Alice provides value to Bob, Bob's account goes negative by that amount and Alice's goes positive. No one needed permission to issue the credit. No one paid interest. The community's willingness to exchange is the backing.

Tools: OpenMRS (open source), Cyclos (cooperative banking software), or a simple GitHub-tracked spreadsheet to start.

### 2. Bitcoin / Lightning
Borderless. Permissionless. No bank can freeze it. No government can easily block it. No credit score required to receive it.

SolarPunk Bitcoin address: `bc1qka74n62h3zk9mcv8v8xjtjtwehmnm24w3pfzzr`

Lightning: coming when the first $20 funds the Strike or Voltage node.

### 3. Community Savings Pool
Members contribute what they can. Pool provides emergency funds when members need it. Transparent ledger. Community-governed rules. No profit extracted.

Currently: $0. Building the infrastructure before the treasury.

### 4. Zero-Interest Emergency Loans Within Network
Network members who need it fast get it fast from the pool. Pay back when you can. No interest. No late fees. No credit check. The community's trust in each other is the collateral.

## Why This Makes "Evil People Angry"

Traditional banks make money by: charging interest (money for having money), charging fees (money for accessing your own money), credit gatekeeping (deciding who gets to participate in the economy at all), and fractional reserve lending (lending out 10x what they actually hold).

This system does none of those things. Every dollar in the SolarPunk pool is one dollar. It earns the community's gratitude, not interest. There's no rent charged on value. There's no middleman extracting their cut from every transaction between a person who has something and a person who needs it.

That's what a new economy looks like. Not an abstraction. This.
